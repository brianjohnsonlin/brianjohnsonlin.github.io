Meet The Team!

Brian Lin: Lead Designer, Developer

Brian Lin is a designer and one of the founders of Eggy Interactive. He likes many different types of games ranging from First-Person Shooters and Role-Playing games to Sandbox and Exploration games.

Nathan Irwin: Lead Producer, Developer

Nathan is a transfer student at UCSC, originally from Davis, CA. He enjoys a broad range of games, with some of his favorites being Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Mirror's Edge, Heroes of the Storm, Overwatch, and the Dark Souls/Bloodborne series.

Ryan Lima: Lead Developer

Ryan really likes exploration in games, ranging from the epic experiences in the Elder Scrolls series to the quiet charm of Proteus.

Anderson Tu: Audio Engineer, Composer, QA Coordinator

Anderson finds a strong passion incorporating his love for music within video games. He finds great joy in working alongside his teammates and looks forward to potentially doing his own work in the future. He enjoys playing games such as Hearthstone and Don't Starve.

Yunyi Ding: Art Director, Artist

Yunyi is a founder of Eggy Interactive. Yunyi is a big fan of games with interesting puzzle elements such as Fez and Broken Age. Being an artist and a designer at heart, her favorite parts in games are art and animation. "A long journey of exploring Chinese calligraphy establishes my aesthetic tendency with the emphasis on Power."
